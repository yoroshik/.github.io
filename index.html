What,s Natto

Do you know Natto is what?

It's the traditional food in Japan. Natto is a fermented food made by fermenting well-steamed soybeans with natto bacteria.
It features a unique smell and a special way of eating.
Some Japanese people don't like it, but do you like it?

First of all, let's talk about how to eat!

There are various ways to eat natto. As so-called natto rice, natto is often placed on cooked white rice and eaten together.

In addition to soy sauce and sauce, it is common to add Japanese sardines. Eggs, green onions, Japanese ginger, grated radish, and seaweed are often eaten together.

It is widely bought and sold mainly in grocery stores equipped with refrigeration facilities such as supermarkets and convenience stores.

The more stir, the more delicious it becomes!

  • The best way to eat natto is to pull a string and knead it well so that it contains air. If you are not accustomed to eating natto, the problems are that the bowl and table are soiled by the peculiar stretched and torn threads, and that it is difficult to eat.

  • TIt is not preferable to add sauce before mixing the natto. It will be over-hydrated and will not be very sticky. As a kind of idea product, a special stick for mixing natto is also sold.

  • Natto is rich in vitamin K and soy-derived protein, and is still a high-quality protein source. It is also rich in dietary fiber. Bacillus natto is called probiotics and is also thought to be useful for the intestinal environment. Natto has an antibacterial effect and has an effect of suppressing the growth of pathogenic Escherichia coli.
